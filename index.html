WHAT IS AIGC

AIGC is a platform that allows users to generate content using artificial intelligence. Users can generate content in the form of text, images, and audio. The content generated can be used for various purposes such as creating a website, writing a book, or even making a movie. The platform is easy to use and requires no technical knowledge. Users can generate content in a matter of minutes.
